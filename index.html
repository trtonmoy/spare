FAQ

What is Spare?

Spare is a Telegram-based wallet tracker bot designed to help you effectively manage your cryptocurrency holdings. Its primary function is to detect and remove the dust - tiny, fragmented balances and unwanted tokens - from your wallet, ensuring your digital assets stay organized and efficient.

How do I Get Spare Bot?

You can easily access Spare Bot by searching for it on Telegram. Once you've found it, simply start a chat with the bot, and you're ready to begin sweeping the dust from your wallet.

Is Spare Bot secure?

Yes, your security and privacy are our top priorities. Spare Bot does not compromise your wallet information or personal data. It operates with strict security measures to ensure your assets remain safe and confidential.

What wallets does Spare Bot support?

Spare Bot is designed to be compatible with a wide range of cryptocurrency wallets. Whether you're using a hardware wallet, a software wallet, or an exchange wallet, Spare Bot can help you manage and declutter your holdings. Q: Can I trust Spare Bot with my funds? A: Spare Bot does not have access to your actual funds. It only provides tracking and dust removal services. Your assets always remain in your full control. Q: How does Spare Bot identify dust in my wallet? A: Spare Bot uses advanced algorithms to detect and categorize dust in your wallet. It then presents you with the option to sweep away these tiny balances and tokens. Q: Do I need to pay for Spare Bot's services? A: Spare Bot may offer both free and premium services, depending on your needs. You can choose the option that suits you best. Q: Is Spare Bot suitable for both beginners and experienced crypto users? A: Absolutely! Spare Bot is designed with a user-friendly interface, making it accessible to both newcomers and seasoned crypto enthusiasts. Its intuitive design ensures a straightforward experience for everyone. Q: How often should I use Spare Bot to clean my wallet? A: The frequency of use depends on your wallet activity and how often you acquire new assets. Regularly checking for dust and using Spare Bot is recommended to maintain a clutter-free wallet. Q: What happens to the dust Spare Bot removes? A: Spare Bot typically converts the dust it removes into a format that can be withdrawn or added to your primary cryptocurrency balance, helping you maximize the value of your holdings. Spare Bot is here to simplify your cryptocurrency management and maintain a clean, efficient wallet. If you have more questions or need assistance, feel free to ask, and we'll be happy to help! 💨💰
